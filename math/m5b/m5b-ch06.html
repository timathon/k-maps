第六单元 Unit 6

分数的加法和减法 Addition & Subtraction of Fractions

学会同分母和异分母分数的加减运算！

Learn how to add and subtract fractions with same or different denominators!

🧠 核心知识点 🧠 Core Concepts

  • 同分母分数加减法：Same Denominator: 分母不变，分子相加减。 Keep the denominator, add/subtract numerators.
    a/c ± b/c = (a ± b) / c
  • 异分母分数加减法：Different Denominators: 先通分（找最小公倍数），再按同分母计算。 Find the LCD (Least Common Denominator), then calculate.
    a/b ± c/d → 先通分LCD First → 再加减Then calculate
  • 带分数加减法：Mixed Numbers:
    • 整数部分和分数部分分别加减。
    • Add/subtract whole numbers and fractions separately.
    • 如果分数部分不够减，需要从整数部分借1。
    • Borrow 1 from the whole number if the fraction part is too small.
  • 计算结果：能约分的要约分成最简分数。假分数要化成带分数或整数。 Results: Simplify to the lowest terms. Convert improper fractions to mixed numbers.
💡 解题秘籍Pro Tips:
1. 结果要约分：计算结果不是最简分数时，一定要约分成最简分数。 1. Always Simplify: Reduce results to the simplest form.
2. 混合运算顺序：和整数一样，先乘除后加减，有括号先算括号。 2. Order of Operations: Follow BEDMAS/PEMDAS as with whole numbers.
异分母加减先通分，结果约分最简形。混合运算同整数，括号优先理分明。 For diff denom find LCD, simplify result to simplest form. Mixed ops like integers, parentheses first to be clear. 异分母加减先通分，结果约分最简形。混合运算同整数，括号优先理分明。 For diff denom find LCD, simplify result to simplest form. Mixed ops like integers, parentheses first to be clear.
