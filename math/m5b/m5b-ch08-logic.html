找次品策略逻辑训练 Finding Defective Items Logic

Master the "divide by three" strategy and build rigorous reasoning skills.

💡 第一关：思维选择 💡 Round 1: Logic Choice

1. 使用天平找次品（次品较轻）时，将待测物品分成几份是最科学、最高效的？ When using a scale to find a lighter defective item, dividing into how many groups is most efficient?
2. 如果有 81 个零件，其中有一个较轻的次品，最少称几次能保证找到它？（逻辑提示：3的几次方大于81） With 81 parts and one lighter defect, what's the minimum weighings to guarantee finding it? (Hint: 3^?)

🧩 第二关：逻辑排序 🧩 Round 2: Logic Sorting

请排列出在 9个 零件中找到一个较轻次品的第一次称量逻辑： Arrange the logic for the first weighing to find one lighter defect among 9 parts:

⣿
从3份中任取2份放到天平两侧。 Place two of the groups on the scale.
⣿
将9个零件平均分成3份，每份3个。 Divide the 9 parts into 3 equal groups of 3.
⣿
若天平不平衡，则次品在较轻的那一端（剩余3个）。 If unbalanced, the defect is in the lighter group.
⣿
若天平平衡，则次品在没上天平的那份中（剩余3个）。 If balanced, the defect is in the group not on the scale.
找次品里分三份，不能均分尽量匀。天平平衡在外面，不平就在重轻边。 For defective items split into three, if not even make it close.
