第八单元 Unit 8

数学广角 · 找次品 Finding the Defective Item

用天平和数学思维，找出那个与众不同的物品！

Use a balance and math thinking to find the one that's different!

🧠 核心知识点 🧠 Core Concepts

  • 什么是次品：What is a Defect: 外观完全相同的一堆物品中，有一个重量与其他不同（偏轻或偏重），这个就是"次品"。 One item among many that looks the same but has a different weight (lighter or heavier).
  • 核心策略：Core Strategy: 将物品尽量平均分成3份，每次称量可以排除最多的可能性。 Divide items into 3 equal (or near-equal) groups to eliminate the most possibilities per weigh.
    最优策略：将待检测物品分成尽量相等的3组 Optimal: Divide items into 3 equal-sized groups.
  • 称量原理：Weighing Principle:
    • 天平平衡 → 次品在没有放上去的那组 Balance Level → Defect is in the group not on the pans
    • 天平不平衡 → 次品在较轻（或较重）的那一边 Balance Tilted → Defect is on the lighter (or heavier) side
  • 最少称量次数：Min Weighs Required:
    物品数量Items 最少称量次数Min Weighs 分组策略Strategy

    4-923, 3, 3

💡 解题秘籍Pro Tips:
1. 分3份原则：每次用天平称一次，都能排除三分之二的可能。所以N个物品最少需要的称量次数满足 3ⁿ ≥ N。 1. Rule of 3: Each weigh eliminates 2/3 of possibilities. For N items, min weighs (n) satisfies 3ⁿ ≥ N.
2. 不能平均分时：多出来的放在第三组（不上天平的那组）。比如8个分成3,3,2。 2. Imperfect Split: Put extras in the 3rd group (off the scale). E.g., 8 items → 3, 3, 2.
3. 画树状图：列出所有可能结果（平衡/左轻/右轻），清晰分析！ 3. Tree Diagrams: List outcomes (Balance/Left/Right) for clear analysis.
找次品里分三份，不能均分尽量匀。天平平衡在外面，不平就在重轻边。 Divide into three groups, keep them as equal as can be. If balanced, it's outside; if tilted, it's on the pans. 找次品里分三份，不能均分尽量匀。天平平衡在外面，不平就在重轻边。 Divide into three groups, keep them as equal as can be. If balanced, it's outside; if tilted, it's on the pans.
