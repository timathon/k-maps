🧠 核心知识点 🧠 Core Concepts

  • 旋转三要素：Three Elements of Rotation:
    • 旋转中心Center of Rotation — 围绕哪个点旋转The fixed point
    • 旋转方向Direction — 顺时针 or 逆时针Clockwise or Counter-clockwise
    • 旋转角度Angle — 转了多少度Degrees rotated
  • 旋转的特征：Characteristics:
    • 旋转前后，图形的形状和大小不变。 Shape and size remain unchanged.
    • 图形上的每个点都绕旋转中心转过相同的角度。 Every point rotates by the same angle.
    • 对应点到旋转中心的距离相等。 Distance from the center remains constant for corresponding points.
  • 画旋转图形的步骤：Steps to Draw:
    1. 找到旋转中心Find the center
    2. 确定关键点Identify key points
    3. 将关键点分别旋转指定角度Rotate points by the specified angle
    4. 按顺序连接新的点Connect the new points in order
  • 旋转对称图形：Rotational Symmetry: 一个图形绕某个点旋转一定角度后能与自身完全重合。如正方形旋转90°、等边三角形旋转120°。 A shape that looks the same after some rotation (e.g., Square at 90°, Triangle at 120°).
💡 解题秘籍Pro Tips:
1. 找对应点：图形旋转后，每个点到旋转中心的距离是不变的。 1. Constant Distance: The distance to the center never changes during rotation.
2. 方向判定：顺时针是往右绕，逆时针是往左绕。 2. Clock Face: Clockwise is right; Counter-clockwise is left.
